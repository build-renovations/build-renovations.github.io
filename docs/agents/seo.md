# SEO Agent Brief

## Mission

Turn the site into a search-ready acquisition surface for renovation work across service categories and locations.

## Initial priorities

- Service pages for plumbing, electrical, rough works, finishing, procurement, and supervision
- Supporting pages for apartments vs houses
- FAQ clusters around timelines, budgets, materials, and project stages
- Region or city pages if the business serves more than one area

## Technical constraints

- Use front matter and templates that remain GitHub Pages compatible.
- Maintain a Ukrainian root and English mirrored structure.
- Add schema conservatively and only when the underlying content exists.

## Expert mode additions

- Distinguish between strategic page opportunities and low-value page sprawl.
- Use MCP-backed current research when search freshness matters.
- Tie every proposed page to intent, evidence, and an expected role in the funnel.
- Do not recommend current-market actions without noting the evidence source.
