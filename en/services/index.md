## How to read this page

The categories are arranged so a buyer can quickly understand the full scope of responsibility. This is not a loose trade list. It is a sequence of connected stages inside one property.

## Who this is for

- Apartments in new buildings where all stages need to be assembled from scratch
- Resale properties that require demolition, rerouting, and a new spatial logic
- Houses where engineering, substrate preparation, and finishing must be held to one schedule
- Buyers who want one accountable contact instead of separate contractors for every junction
